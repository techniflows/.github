# techniflows Inc.

**techniflows Inc.** builds privacy-first AI systems for video, on-device AI, and edge environments.

We design technology around a simple belief: **technology should belong to everyone, not just a few.**  
Our work focuses on helping people and organizations keep control over their own data through practical AI systems that run close to where data is created — in browsers, on devices, on-premise, or at the edge.

## What we build

- [**VideoMasker**](https://www.videomasker.com/) — AI-powered video de-identification for privacy-sensitive media
- **CCTV intelligence** — edge AI for video search, analysis, and selective anonymization
- **On-device AI** — local AI processing that reduces cloud dependency and protects data sovereignty
- **Lightweight AI infrastructure** — tools and automation for small GPU, edge, and on-premise AI environments

## Our approach

At techniflows, we care about AI that works beyond demos.

We build systems that are technically sound, operationally realistic, and simple enough to use in real environments. Our products are designed for situations where sensitive visual data, privacy, reliability, and accessibility matter.

Guided by our core values — **Form, Function, and Flow** — we focus on:

- **Form** — solid technical structure and reliable architecture
- **Function** — practical products that solve real problems
- **Flow** — technology that creates value and remains accessible to more people
